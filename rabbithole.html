The workshop floor.

These pages are not part of the public site. They are the test benches, type explorations, design proofs, and internal tools that preceded it. Each one reflects a decision made; a question asked before the answer was settled.

Showcase · Five entries

01 — typography

Type Lab

Eighteen typographic presets explored in a live canvas. Font pairs, optical size, leading, measure, and accent colour: all adjustable, all visible at once. The tool used to settle the site's heading and body choices.

Enter 02 · process

Review Canvas

The client-facing review interface. Tabbed, full-viewport, with a description pane for each frame. This is the environment in which clients are shown work in progress before a single revision note is written.

Enter 03 · identity

Vitruvian System

The studio mark in its full context: section dividers, stat-rail watermarks, corner plaques, and footer centrepieces. Five motif variants and the logic for choosing between them.

Enter 04 · motion

Vitruvian in Motion

Five orbital variants rendered in continuous motion. Concentric geometry, counter-rotating layers, and a slow breath cycle that the eye takes a moment to locate. The mark doing what it was made for.

Enter 05 · layout

Hero Explorations

Three hero configurations considered before the current layout was chosen. The decisions recorded here are as instructive as the outcome: what was tried, what was discarded, and why.

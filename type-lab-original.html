Precision in service of beauty.

Every line of code serves a purpose. Every pixel is intentional. This is what a site looks like when the margins have been earned and the letterforms have been chosen, not defaulted to.

A static site is not a limitation — it is a posture. It says the words matter more than the scripts, that readers deserve a page that loads in the time it takes them to blink. Typography is the first promise a page makes: that somebody cared before you arrived.

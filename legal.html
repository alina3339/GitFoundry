01 - Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Last updated: March 2026

Data controller: GitFoundry, United Kingdom

Contact: contact@gitfoundry.co.uk

What we collect

When you use our brief form or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Business or brand name
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Your website vision, style preferences, and project requirements
  • Domain name (if you have one)

How we use your data

  • Respond to enquiries and provide project quotes
  • Deliver web design and development services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Comply with legal obligations

Lawful basis for processing

  • Legitimate interest: responding to enquiries and delivering services
  • Contract: processing data to fulfil service agreements
  • Consent: voluntary submission through forms

Who we share data with

  • Email service providers
  • GitHub (for deployment, no personal data stored)
  • Domain registrars (if requested)

Data retention

Project data is retained for the duration of our relationship and up to 24 months after completion. Enquiry data is retained for 12 months. You may request deletion at any time.

International transfers

Data is primarily processed in the UK. Third-party services outside the UK, such as GitHub (US), have appropriate safeguards in place.

02 - Cookie Policy

Cookies and tracking

No analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or ads are used by default. GitHub Pages may set strictly necessary technical cookies.

Third-party services

Google Fonts may log IP addresses as part of their service delivery. No other third-party scripts are loaded.

Cookies on client sites

If you request Google Analytics or other integrations, appropriate consent mechanisms are implemented.

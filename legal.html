The legal documents that govern GitFoundry's services. Written in plain English wherever possible.

Privacy policy · Last updated April 2026

Who we are

GitFoundry is a UK-based web studio operated by Azhar, based in Stoke, Staffordshire. For any privacy-related enquiry, contact info@gitfoundry.co.uk.

What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to respond to your enquiry and deliver your project:

  • Your name and email address (from the project brief form)
  • Details you provide about your project
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics (anonymised IP, browser type, pages visited)

How we use it

Personal data is used only to reply to your enquiry, deliver the service you've commissioned, and comply with our legal obligations (e.g. tax records). We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except where required by law.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at info@gitfoundry.co.uk and we will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Enquiry data is retained for 12 months unless a project proceeds, in which case project records are retained for 6 years to meet UK tax requirements.

Terms of service · Last updated April 2026

Scope

These terms apply to all website design and development services provided by GitFoundry to clients in the UK and internationally.

Quotes & pricing

All prices are quoted in GBP and include VAT where applicable. Quotes are valid for 30 days. The fee covers the scope described in the original brief — additional work is agreed separately.

Payment

A 50% deposit is due on project start. The remaining 50% is due on handover of the completed site. Projects are not deployed to production until final payment clears.

Revisions

Each pricing tier includes a defined number of revision rounds. Additional revision rounds are charged at £45/hour.

Ownership

On final payment, the complete source code is transferred to the client. The client owns all custom design work, content, and code produced specifically for their project.

Third-party services

Where a project uses third-party services (GitHub Pages, Formspree, domain registrars), the client is responsible for maintaining those accounts after handover.

Liability

Our liability is limited to the fees paid for the specific project. We cannot be held liable for business losses, third-party service outages, or issues arising from client-made changes post-handover.

Cookie policy · Last updated April 2026

Cookies we use

This site uses cookies only for Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics cookies are optional and only set if you accept them via our cookie banner.

Managing cookies

You can decline cookies via the banner on first visit, or change your choice at any time by clearing your site data in your browser. Declining cookies does not affect your ability to use the site.

No advertising or tracking

We do not use advertising cookies, remarketing pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

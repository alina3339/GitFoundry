Case study — Will4Life

From zero online presence
to a live, professional site.

The situation
Renjith Reghu was launching Will4Life — a new UK startup — and needed a professional website that could communicate the business clearly and credibly to prospective clients from day one. He had a clear vision but no existing web presence to build from.
What I built
A clean, professionally designed site reflecting the Will4Life brand — built from scratch, no templates, no drag-and-drop builders. Deployed to GitHub Pages with a custom domain. Full source code handed over to Renjith on completion. Hosting cost to him since launch: £0.
The handover
The full GitFoundry packet — live site, Git repository transferred to Renjith's account, a plain-English owner's manual specific to Will4Life, a 30-minute walkthrough recording, a one-page emergency runbook, a migration playbook, and a 90-day support window. He could operate the site himself from day one. See the sample packet →
The outcome
The site has been live since early 2026 — fast, accessible, and costing nothing to run. Renjith reported strong positive feedback from contacts and clients who viewed it after launch. In his own words: "a product much beyond my expectations." Visit will4life.co.uk →

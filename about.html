About

The person behind the build.

GitFoundry is not an agency. No account managers, no handoffs, no junior teams. A UK studio supporting local businesses — every site built by the same person from brief to handover.

Who

Hi, I'm Azhar.
I design websites that you'll
still own in ten years.

Background
I'm based in Stoke, Staffordshire, and I work with local UK businesses and clients across the country. My background is in software and digital design, with a focus on quality, performance, and precision. GitFoundry grew out of a straightforward frustration: most people building a professional presence online end up paying rent indefinitely for something they never truly own. I wanted to offer a better arrangement — and to do it for UK independents who deserve proper craft.
Why this
There is no shortage of ways to build a website. Squarespace, Wix, Webflow — they all work. But they all charge you monthly, they all hold the assets, and they all reserve the right to change pricing, alter features, or suspend your account. What I offer is the alternative: a site designed from scratch, hosted free on GitHub Pages, and handed over to you in full. You own every file. There is no platform to abandon you.
Supporting local
UK independents, local businesses, and professionals The UK high street is built on independent shops, studios, trades, and professional practices. They deserve web presences as considered as their work — not rented templates that look identical to everyone else's. That's who this studio is for.
Location
Stoke, Staffordshire, UK. I work with clients remotely across the country. The brief process is handled entirely online — no calls required unless you'd prefer one.
How the work is done

Methodical, not rushed.
Every site earns its handover.

A site isn't shown to a client because a deadline arrived. It's shown because it's ready. That distinction matters.

The standard
Lighthouse audit on every project Before any site is shared with a client, it passes a Google Lighthouse audit across all four metrics — performance, accessibility, best practices, and SEO. This isn't a checkbox. It's the threshold for handover.
The approach
Multiple iterations before you see anything Every project goes through several rounds of internal refinement before a preview is shared. Layout, typography, contrast, responsiveness, and performance are all reviewed before a client's time is asked for.
The handover
Full source code, not just a live URL You receive the complete repository — every file, every commit. You can take it to another developer, edit it yourself, or simply leave it running on GitHub Pages indefinitely. There is no ongoing dependency on me.
The relationship
You deal with the person building your site There are no account managers between us. When you ask a question, the person who answers is the same person designing and building your site. That keeps decisions fast, communication honest, and quality consistent.

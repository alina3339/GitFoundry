Scope & boundaries

What GitHub Pages supports — and what it doesn't.

GitHub Pages serves static files. This covers the vast majority of professional website needs.

Supported
Local business sites · Portfolio sites · CV & résumé pages · Business landing pages · Research publications · Documentation · Event pages · Non-profit homepages · Professional profiles · Writer sites · Contact forms (via Formspree or Netlify Forms)
Not supported
Server-side databases · User login systems · E-commerce checkout · Real-time feeds · User-generated content · CMS-driven content management
If your project needs any of these, we'll discuss the right approach before quoting.

How it works

From brief to live
in under two weeks.

You deal with the same person throughout — the one designing and building your site. No handoffs, no agency layers, no surprises.

01
Brief

You fill in a short form about your goals, audience, and taste. No calls unless you want one. Most people finish it in under 10 minutes.

02
Design & build

The same person who read your brief builds your site. Within 5 working days you’ll get a private preview link.

03
Revisions

You live with the preview, gather your thoughts, and we refine it together. Each package includes clear revision rounds so nothing drags on.

04
Handover

We connect your domain, make the site public on GitHub Pages, and send you the complete source files. From that moment it belongs to you — no strings.
